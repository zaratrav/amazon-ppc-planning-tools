Amazon PPC Best Practices

Budget Allocation Strategy

Most sellers allocate 10-30% of revenue to PPC. New sellers should start conservatively (5-10%) and scale as they prove profitability. Always set daily budgets based on profit margins, not just revenue goals. A $1,000 sale with 20% ACoS requires careful margin calculation.

Optimal CPC Bidding

Your CPC bid should be 1/3 to 1/2 of your profit per sale. If you make $30 profit per sale, bid $10-15 CPC. Start with keyword research data, then adjust based on actual performance. Category CPCs vary wildly: electronics average $1-3, supplements $0.50-1.50, luxury items $5-20+.

Campaign Funnel Optimization

Monitor your funnel at each stage: Impressions → Clicks (CTR) → Orders (Conversion Rate). A typical healthy funnel: 0.5% CTR + 5-10% conversion = 0.025-0.05% final conversion. If your CTR is low, improve your ad copy and keywords. If conversion is low, fix your listing and pricing.

ACoS & Profitability

ACoS (Advertising Cost of Sale) tells you what percentage of revenue goes to ads. Break-even ACoS = (100% - profit margin). If you have 30% margin, your break-even ACoS is 70%. Target 20-30% ACoS for profitability. First-page rankings often justify 50%+ ACoS due to long-term organic benefits.

Testing & Scaling

Always test before scaling. Start with 5-10 keywords in Automatic campaigns. Track performance for 2 weeks minimum. Once you identify winners (ACoS under target), increase bids by 5-10%. Scale budget 20-30% monthly until you hit saturation or margin targets.
